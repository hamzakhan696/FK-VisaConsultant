Tourist Visa

The world is a wonderful place to visit and live. Every country has its hidden gems of beauty. A type of non-immigrant visa called a "tourist" or "temporary resident" visa enables the holder to visit a foreign nation for a little time and then temporarily stay there. A tourist’s visa application may be made for any of the following reasons: vacation, getting together with family and friends, pleasure, or receiving medical treatment. Depending on the country one wishes to apply to, the length of a tourist visa will vary. Tourist visas are often issued for a duration of three to six months, though this can vary depending on your profile and the nation you choose to travel to.
